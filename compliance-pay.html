Understanding Your Pay Package

Competitive Pay That Reflects Your Flexibility

Travel therapy and education roles typically come with higher pay than permanent positions. Because these assignments are short-term and require adaptability, facilities offer strong compensation to attract skilled professionals ready to make a difference.

Perks That Go Beyond the Basics

In addition to your hourly rate or stipend, many contracts include added benefits like housing assistance, travel reimbursement, bonuses, and access to insurance. These extras are designed to support your lifestyle and ease your transition into new settings.

Personalized Support to Maximize Your Package

Your total compensation depends on factors like your role, specialty, and location. Your recruiter will advocate for you every step of the way—helping you secure the best possible pay and benefits to match your goals, priorities, and where you’re headed next.
